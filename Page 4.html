    • Here you will find some popular cooking games to play.

    Cooking Fever is a game where people cook food for customers and gain points if they make enough food. Points are given in star form.

    In Papa's Pizzeria, you play as Roy or his sister Joy. You work at a Pizzeria, serving up delicious pizzas for lots of customers while Papa Louie leaves on his vacation.

    In Papa's Freezeria, you play as Penny or Alberto and you get to run an ice cream parlor. Papa Louie leaves again on another vaction, leaving you stuck on Calypso Island serving ice cream sundaes to many customers.
