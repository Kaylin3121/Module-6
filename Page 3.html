    The Game of Life

    In The Game of Life, you start with colored cars, moving around a gameboard trying to retire. You also can choose which path you want to take as well. You can either Go to College or Get a Job. The goal of this game is to teach everyone about life and how to retire at the end of their work life. I have played this game numerous times over the years, and its one of my favorite games to play.
