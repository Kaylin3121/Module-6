    Tetris

    Tetris is a game where you place different colored blocks on a plastic board to try and make a match. If you make more matches than the other players, you win.

    The Tetris Movie

    In 2023, the movie about Tetris came out. It tells the story about how the game's creator had to fight with Russian forces over the game's rights. The trailer is below.

    Jenga

    During Jenga, you must remove the blocks from the tower without making it fall over. If it falls you lose, but if you manage to remove more blocks than the other players, then you win.
