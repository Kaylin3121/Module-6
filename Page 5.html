    The Muppet Show

    The Muppet Show was a 5 season tv show that ran from 1976 to 1981. It featured Kermit as the host of the show, and he welcomed many celebrity guests onto the show during its 5 year run.

    Muppet Babies

    Muppet Babies was an ABC show. It ran from 1984 to 1991 for eight seasons. By 1990, Jim Henson's health had declined rapidly, and he passed away on May 16th, 1990 from Pnumonia complications.

    The Jim Henson Hour

    The Jim Henson Hour was a mixed bag in my opinion. It ran for one season in 1989, but reruns ran until after Jim's passing until 1992. It contained behind the scenes clips from many Muppet productions, and it also was Jim's final show he ever starred in.
